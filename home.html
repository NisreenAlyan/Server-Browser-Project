Simple Browser
What is it?
Simple Browser is a lightweight web-scripted interface to browse the World Wide Web. It is purposefully built to be simple and easy to use. It is used in programs requiring a interface design over a normal web browser (such as Internet Explorer) and is available as a standalone application (running through Internet Explorer/Safari).
Where is it?
Simple Browser is available, for FREE, at www.webventures.com.au/sb/. It is also available at our Sourceforge.net pages. There is also a backup website at http://sbrowser.sourceforge.net/". Simple Browser is also inbuilt in Simple Desktop, A free online desktop.
Who created it?
Simple Browser was created by Tristan Roberts.
